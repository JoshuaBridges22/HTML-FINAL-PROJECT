Mission statement

Come to The Cold Camp and have an experience like no other campsite.

Daring the cold can be daunting but with a little bit of courage you'll find that this may be the best camping experience you'll ever have. From the unique wildlife in this Frosty enviroment to the ice cold swim you'll quickly warm up too.
