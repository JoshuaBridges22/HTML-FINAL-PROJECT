Events

When coming to Cold Camp, our staff plans fun events for our campers to attend if they are interested

SnowBank Blues


A little before sundown we ask campers who want to attend to come to the banks and listen to our wonderful staff play some nice blues and they also take request other than blues

Polar Bear

In our warmer weather, At sunrise, We invite our Campers to come join us and Jump into the lake for a very thorough wake up call

MORE TO COME

Our staff is always planning new events for our Campers so they can have the best Experience at Cold Camp.
